## Tips for a Successful Interview

**DO**:
- ✅ Listen more than you talk (70/30 rule)
- ✅ Take detailed notes
- ✅ Ask "why" to understand deeper motivations
- ✅ Paraphrase to confirm understanding
- ✅ Show enthusiasm for their business
- ✅ Be honest about what's possible
- ✅ Set realistic expectations

**DON'T**:
- ❌ Jump to solutions before understanding problems
- ❌ Use technical jargon
- ❌ Promise features you're not sure about
- ❌ Commit to unrealistic timelines
- ❌ Skip the "boring" questions about budget/timeline
- ❌ Forget to ask about competitors
- ❌ Rush through it

**If You Get Stuck**:
- "Can you tell me more about that?"
- "Why is that important to you?"
- "What would that look like ideally?"
- "Help me understand..."
- "I want to make sure I get this right..."

**Remember**: The goal of this interview is to deeply understand your client's needs, not to show off your technical knowledge. Ask questions, listen actively, and build trust.
